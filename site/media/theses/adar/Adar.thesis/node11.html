Background

  In this chapter we will review previous efforts in information system research that may prove useful for the type of hybrid-search capabilities we will attempt in Haystack. Specifically, we will approach the previous work from four directions: unstructured search technologies, structured search technology, associative search technology, and current hybrid solutions. Finally, in Section  we will discuss the two primary building blocks for this thesis, Haystack and Lore.
